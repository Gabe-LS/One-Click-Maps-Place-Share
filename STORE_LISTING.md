# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Short description (132 chars max)

Replaces the Google Maps share button: copies place name, location and short link to clipboard.

## Detailed description

One click. Place name, location, and short link — copied to your clipboard.

When you click the share button on a Google Maps place, this extension skips the share dialog and instantly copies three lines to your clipboard:

    Kota Bharu Sultan Ismail Petra Airport
    Pengkalan Chepa, 16100 Kota Bharu, Kelantan, Malaysia
    https://maps.app.goo.gl/1XdC7ozQWLMGD6Zn8

Paste it into a message, note, or document. No extra clicks, no dialog, no menus.

Features:
- Copies place name, address, and short share link in one click
- Works in any language — tested across English, Chinese, Hindi, Spanish, French, Arabic, Bengali, Portuguese, Russian, and Japanese
- No data collection, no analytics, no external servers
- Optional debug logging for troubleshooting (off by default)

The second click on the same place is instant — the share link is cached locally until you navigate to a different place.
